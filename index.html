About the Conference

The Indian financial sector has undergone a dynamic transformation over the last decade. The roots of digital transformation however can be traced to the launch of Aadhaar, a unique, unified identification system which now is linked in any kind of benefit transfer of the government. Aadhaar, along with the launch of Jan Dhan Yojana in 2014 linked with mobile phone numbers, gave the government a unique tool like JAM. The key players in the sector like Banks, NBFCs and service companies have been capitalizing on technology to generate value and transform India's financial ecosystem. Supported by a surge in digital adoption and the startup revolution, India today supports the 3rd largest fintech ecosystem. Trends in the financial sector in India if observed with a bird's eye are catching up the pace of the international trends. But, finally, the conference will provide a unique platform of discussion and discourse with the experts and learners predominantly from academics through their presentation of papers and also with the few invited experts from the various sub-sectors who are in practice.

About the Department

Vidyasagar University is a reputed University in the Eastern region of India which has been catering to the needs of higher education since 1985. The Department of Commerce which is among the oldest departments in the varsity presently offers M.Com. and Ph.D degrees. The department seven full-time faculty members, each of whom have a Doctoral degree. On an annual basis, the department regularly publishes its journal, "Vidyasagar University Journal of Commerce" bearing ISSN 0973-5917 which is indexed with Citefactor.org and EBSCO. The twenty seventh volume of the journal will be released very soon. The department also regularly organizes special lectures and has also conducted three research methodology workshops. This year it will organize the twenty eighth annual conference.
